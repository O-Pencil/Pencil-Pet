# Pencil-Pet Optimized Image Generation Prompts

These prompts are specifically tuned for your ginger-and-white cat to maintain consistency across all states. Use these in DALL-E 3, Midjourney (v6), or Stable Diffusion.

## 1. Character Identity (Always include this)
> **[Character_ID]**: A cute ginger and white bicolor domestic shorthair cat. It has a distinctive white inverted "V" mask on its face, covering the muzzle and extending up between the eyes to the forehead. The top of the head and ears are bright orange tabby. It has golden-green eyes and a pink nose. The body has a bright orange saddle on the back with white chest and four white paws. The tail is long and orange with subtle tabby rings.

## 2. Global Style (Always include this)
> **[Style]**: 2D digital illustration, retro early-2000s browser pet game style, Flash game animal icon aesthetic. Clean, smooth, slightly thick dark outlines. Simple flat colors with soft, minimal gentle blending. Isolated on a PURE WHITE BACKGROUND. No shadows, no floor, no background elements. High-quality vector-like art.

## 3. Specific State Prompts

### Resting (Loaf Pose)
> **Prompt**: [Character_ID] in a "loaf" posture, paws tucked under body, creating a rounded compact silhouette. Eyes peacefully closed. [Style]

### Sleeping (Side Pose)
> **Prompt**: [Character_ID] lying on its side, body slightly curled, tail resting near paws, deep sleep expression. [Style]

### Working (Focused)

### Stretching (Transition)
> **Prompt**: [Character_ID] performing a deep stretch, front paws extended far forward, chest low, back arched high, cute yawn. [Style]

### Held (Dangling)
> **Prompt**: [Character_ID] dangling naturally as if picked up by armpits, paws hanging down, neutral surprised expression. [Style]

### Happy (Waving)
> **Prompt**: [Character_ID] standing on hind legs, waving one front paw, wide happy smile, sparkling eyes. [Style]

---

## Tips for Best Results:
1. **Consistency**: Always keep the `[Character_ID]` and `[Style]` exactly the same.
2. **Background**: Even though you want transparency, asking for "Pure White Background" usually yields cleaner results for background removal tools.
3. **Aspect Ratio**: Use `--ar 1:1` for square assets.
